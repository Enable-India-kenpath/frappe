What is Purple Registry platform?

Disability Innovative Solutions Hub is a unifying platform where people with disabilities and stakeholders can search for assistive solutions. An assistive solution can either be a product, process or program that helps a person with disabilities to overcome their barrier.

Why Purple Registry

The disability sector works in silos. The main drawback is that the inventor's time and effort is not being adequately utilized to address needs of persons with disabilities. With Purple Registry, people are able to search, develop, replicate and fund emerging solutions based on the needs of the community.

How does Purple Registry work?

Purple Registry is an AI based search engine for people with disabilities and stakeholders to identify solutions for creation, replication, scaling and marketing. The user on entering a problem statement will get multiple options on how to solve the problem. The solutions could be high, medium or low tech in nature.

Who does Purple Registry help?

Purple Registry is helpful for all the stakeholders associated with the disability sector like people with disabilities, parents, families, caregivers, accelerators, start ups and investors.
